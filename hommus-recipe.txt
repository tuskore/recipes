1 400g can of chickpeas, Drained and rinsed
2 tbspns of  tahini
3 Cloves of garlic crushed
Juice of one lemon
1/4 cup of water
2 and 1/2 tblspns of olive oil
1/2 tsp of sea salt

Combine everything in a food processor. May take 2-3 mins until smooth and no lumps.

Enjoy with toasted Leb bread or vegetables.
